Upon conducting a market research, I could not find a single website that had the capability of what we currently have to offer and its potential in the near future. More specifically, www.moneyabcs.org provides a platform that combines the winning aspects of Twitter, Pinterest and BlackBoard with the base topic of Financial Literacy into one visually attractive and user friendly page. After a quick demo on moving along the page, the landing page displays current articles with the spiciest headlines tempting the visitor to stay engaged with the information on finance related topics. But what brings people to this specific website when compared to other giants such as YouTube? Majority of the videos uploaded by the public onto YouTube have been embedded on the landing page itself of this website, therefore, making this website a very effective and engaging financial literacy micro-database. From a business perspective, MoneyABCs.com solves an existing issue within the community of a single user friendly go-to place for personalized information in personal finance and financial literacy communicated at a personal level.
